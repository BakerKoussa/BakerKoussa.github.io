A Day As

Choosing the future job is a crucial decision for a student to take, as it affects to how the student going to continue his second half of his life. In this project, I collected data for whole Lebanese market and university to list all job and how to be able to reach this position. The objective of this work is to assist the students to reach their dream job where they can work more effectively. I used the full power of Google technologies available and career test website to present a friendly user application. The result of this work will be presented in an IOS/Android mobile application
